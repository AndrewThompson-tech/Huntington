AR
    Autoregressive: Make predictions based on past values, or lags. ​
    AR(p) where p is the optimized number of lags.​
    Long term memory model​

MA
    Moving Average: The current value of a series depends past error terms​
    Shocks that happened long ago have little effect on tomorrow's prediction​
    MA(q) where q is the optimized number of errors​
    Short term memory model​

    Assumes data is stationary​

ARIMA
    Lags + error lags ​
    ARIMA(p, d, q)​
    AR terms, difference, MA terms

ARMAX
    ARIMA model with explanatory variables​
    ARMAX(p, q, b) refers to a model with p autoregressive terms, q moving average terms and b exogenous inputs terms
